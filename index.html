HACKATHON




Collaborative Experiments

Nature and the low background environment provides the opportunity to explore how our energetic body responds. We hope to have access to quick mechanisms to test some of our theories and gauge the edge of theoretical discussions. In the world of biofield and energetics we start with various forms of analog amplification. For this purpose thus far we can utilize the MCP6N11, INA333, AD8221, LMP7701 and OPA1612, OPA2134. Then, for catching and analyzing the signal we can utilize microcontrollers and SBCs, in this case the Raspberry Pi 5, Orange Pi 3, ESp32-S6, ESP8266 and Teensy4.1 as well as various ADC analog and digital sensors: LM337 , ADS1256, GS2200 and HX711. Additionally the good folks at the local electrical and hardware store can supply any course supplies we might need. Muscles, nerves, branches, leaves, trees and mushrooms, are all moving surprising volume of energy.
